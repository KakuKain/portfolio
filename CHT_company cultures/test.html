Commander(GSS)

    • Master COVID-19 situation in CTCI Group's global operations

    • Command and dispatch CTCI group’s COVID-19 prevention operations

    • Release internal and external information related to COVID-19 prevention

    • Supervise and coordinate the operation of COVID-19 prevention center

    • Legal & Compliance

      • Supervise group's operation management and impact assessment

      • Contract force majeure clause evaluation and response

      • Supervision of project insurance claims

    • HR

      • Changes in labor conditions due to COVID-19 prevention, planning labor rights response plans

      • Travel and mobilization information management

      • Cooperate with leave of absence for COVID-19 prevention

    • PSA

      • Supervise the inventory and management of COVID-19 prevention materials at the project sites

      • Supervise site staff's attendance, leave, and medical conditions

    • AGS&PR

      • Submit Group COVID-19 Daily Situation Report

      • Integrate group COVID-19 prevention administrative information ( including quarantine information such as border management, traffic, blockade, etc.)

      • Coordination of group COVID-19 prevention materials

      • Supervision of COVID-19 prevention management in the offices of each companies

      Healthcare Center

      • Control and report of epidemic regulations

      • Propaganda and communication for COVID-19 prevention

      • Concerned cases management and tracking

      • Contact with health-related organizations

      Other related supporting dept.

      Finance Dept. / Corporate Administration Dept. / Procurement Division

      • Assess Operation Management with Legal & Compliance dept. IT Division


      HSE Dept.

      • Supervise epidemic control and prevention actions in project site
